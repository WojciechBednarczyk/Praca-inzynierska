\begin{abstract}
Thesis presents web application aimed at the local community interested in fitness training. The application is designed to facilitate cooperation between the personal trainer and the mentee by functionalities such as sending messages between users and creating training plan for the mentee. The application is intended for personal trainers and their mentees, regardless of their level of advancement.

The beginning of the thesis was devoted to designing the application, defining its requirements and use cases. The third chapter describes the technologies used during the implementation of the application. The fourth chapter is devoted to implementation details. The next chapter describes the graphical user interface offered by the application. The sixth chapter describes the application tests. The last chapter contains a summary of the whole thesis.

\end{abstract}
